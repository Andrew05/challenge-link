Build and participate in location-based challenges effortlessly.
A Hack Western 2015 project

Inspiration

Challenge Link was inspired by the desire to have fun. Scavenger hunts, tours, and geocaching are all location-based challenges that require lots of planning, resources, and setup. We made it effortless with our app: to build a challenge, simply go to a location, press a button to geo-tag it, and then move on to the next one. Your locations will be saved, and when someone participates in the challenge, clues are automatically sent to their phone once they make it to the correct destination. Easy!

It gets even cooler when Pebble gets involved. With your Pebble smartwatch, stay on the move and get the clues right to your wrist. Track your progress, and best of all: feel your progress with vibrations scaled to your distance from the destination.

Created by Umar Azhar, Cathy Chen, Andrew Lawrence, and Albert Tai.
